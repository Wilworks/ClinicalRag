About EvidAnce
EvidAnce is a multi-source clinical evidence agent. It searches PubMed, Europe PMC, Semantic Scholar, and OpenAlex in parallel, scores results by relevance, evidence tier, recency, and West Africa applicability, then synthesises an answer grounded in that evidence.

The name is deliberate — Evidence + Guidance, built for West African clinical contexts.
