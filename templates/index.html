About Evidance
Evidance retrieves real PubMed abstracts for every question, embeds them locally with MiniLM-L6, retrieves the most relevant chunks using FAISS vector search, and grounds the answer strictly in that evidence.

The name is deliberate — Evidence + Guidance, built for West African clinical contexts. Every answer includes confidence scoring and direct PubMed source links.
